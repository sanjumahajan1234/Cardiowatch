AHA/ACC Classification Reference

2017 ACC/AHA Hypertension Guideline — Whelton et al., JACC 2018

Category Systolic Diastolic Action
Normal < 120 and < 80 Annual review; promote healthy habits
Elevated 120–129 and < 80 Lifestyle changes; recheck in 3–6 months
Stage 1 HTN 130–139 or 80–89 Lifestyle + consider medication; recheck in 1 month
Stage 2 HTN ≥ 140 or ≥ 90 Start medication; urgent follow-up within 1 week
Crisis > 180 or > 120 Refer to emergency care immediately
Classification uses the higher-severity rule: a reading is placed in the highest category triggered by either the systolic or diastolic value.
